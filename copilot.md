## **KEY IMPLEMENTATION INSIGHTS**

**1. Company Context Must Permeate the System**
Every component—from voice agents to metrics to objections—must change based on company selection. This is not just UI theming; it's fundamental business logic differentiation.

**2. B2B Sales Cycles Are Much Longer Than B2C**
- Eiger Marvel: 14-30 days discovery to pilot
- SDC Tech AI: 60-90 days discovery to PoC
Your training must reflect this extended timeline and multiple decision-makers.

**3. Enterprise Sales Requires Multiple Stakeholders**
For SDC Tech AI, you're simultaneously training salespeople to engage IT Managers, CFOs, and Operations leaders. Each has different concerns. The system must coach differently based on who's in the room.

**4. ROI Becomes the Conversation**
Both companies live and die by ROI conversations. Build financial calculators into the system that salespeople can reference during calls.

**5. Objection Handling is Company-Specific**
- Eiger Marvel hears "too expensive" and "team adoption fears"
- SDC Tech AI hears "no proven track record" and "implementation risk"
The responses are completely different.
